About the Orchestra

The Gallery Community Orchestra is open to musicians of all ages. Whether you're a primary, intermediate or secondary school student, a teenager, a university student, an adult, or an adult returning to music, you're welcome here.

Rehearsals cover a range of repertoire from classical to contemporary, chosen to be engaging and achievable for players of varying abilities. The orchestra performs at the annual Gallery concert and occasionally at community events throughout the year.

There are two groups: Orchestra and Ensemble, our Junior Orchestra.
